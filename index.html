About the Campaign

Farber Hebrew Day School – Yeshivat Akiva is an nursery through 12th grade Modern Orthodox Zionist Jewish day school committed to providing both a comprehensive Jewish as well as secular education in a vibrant atmosphere of Torah values and academic challenge.

FHDS seeks to imbue its students with a love for Jewish heritage, ...the State of Israel and its people, together with an intense commitment to the Torah way of life. The FHDS graduate will be invested with the desire to contribute significantly to our society as an educated productive citizen and an erudite Torah scholar, and display a strong and real bond with the religious Zionist movement.


FHDS strives to provide opportunities for each student to reach his or her maximum religious and intellectual potential as well as develop the highest standards of personal character.

FHDS ensures its students strive to LIVE a life filled with purpose and a steadfast commitment to the Torah way of life, LEARN in the classroom and beyond as committed life-long learners, and LEAD in our community and in the world.

We credit our strong historical presence within Detroit’s educational landscape to our parents and students who remain committed to the FHDS mission as a Modern Orthodox, Zionist, college-preparatory Jewish Day School.
